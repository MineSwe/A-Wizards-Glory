Player Guide

Title Screen

When starting the game you'll be presented with a Play button, a Endless toggle and a quit button.

Normal Mode

The Normal Mode can be played by untoggling the Endless toggle and pressing Play. It provides a set amount of rounds that are handcrafted with difficulty escalation in mind with a win condition after you've completed all the rounds.

Endless Mode

The Endless Mode can be played by toggling the Endless toggle and pressing Play. It randomizes enemies spawns and spawns them faster and faster until you die where you'll get a high score.

Player Controls

Once you've pressed play and watched the cutscene you'll be put into Opela arena with enemies spawning that are trying to kill you. You need to use your movement and spell casting skills to survive.

Movement

Use WASD to move around and the mouse to look around. You can also jump with rockets to flee from enemies.

Shooting

To actually win the game you need to kill enemies. Toggle the spells by pressing the number keys and you should see a spell appearing around your character. Press the left mouse button to fire off the spell.

Tip: Every spell recasts in the background. So switching spells is an effective way to maximize damage.
